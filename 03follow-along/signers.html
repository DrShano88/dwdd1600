Who Were the Signers?

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous signers of the Declaration of Independance include

Thomas Jefferson
Thomas Jefferson (1743 to 1826) was a Founding Father, principal author of the Declaration of Independence (1776), and the third U.S. President (1801 to 1809). A leader in the Democratic-Republican party, he famously championed liberty, authorized the Louisiana Purchase, and founded the University of Virginia, while also being a lifelong enslaver of people.
John Adams
John Adams, a remarkable political philosopher, served as the second President of the United States (1797-1801), after serving as the first Vice President under President George Washington.
